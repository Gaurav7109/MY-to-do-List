Help

    For writing a task, write at "enter new task" and than either press enter or click on the plus(+) sign.

    If the particular task is finished, then click on the checkbox.

    If you want to delete the finished task, then click on the "Remove the completed task"

    All the deleted tasks are shown on the "Completed Tasks" table.

    If you have deleted any task mistakenly, then it would be present on the "Completed Tasks" table. Just write that task again at "enter new task".

    You can find all your lists by simply clicking on the hamburger icon(top-left) and then click on the "My-List".

    You can create different lists for different tasks writing the title of the list at "enter new list", and then click on the newly created list to write some tasks.

    Try to finish all the tasks before the deadline.

    I wish it was helpful for you. You can provide the suggestion/improvement by going to the contact section

    All the best and have a nice day.
